About Me


Mari is an artist from Chicago who loves working with watercolors. She mostly works figuratively but also enjoys abstraction.

During her time studing Fine Arts at Loyola University, she became interested in how color in art can evoke specific emotions. She first explored this in a series of painting based on the three movements of Beethoven's Moonlight Sonata.

Mari is continuing to explore more creative outlets including sculpture and web design.
